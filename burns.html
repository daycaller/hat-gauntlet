HOW IT WORKS

  • The community vault is a wallet pre-funded with $WIF2 tokens. You can contribute by sending $WIF2 to the vault address above.
  • Every tap in the game adds to the burn pool: 0.020 $WIF2 per anonymous tap, 0.080 $WIF2 per holder tap (4× boost).
  • When the pool reaches 10,000 $WIF2, an admin executes the burn — tokens are sent from the vault to Solana's official burn address. The transaction is published below for everyone to verify on Solscan.
  • The pool resets to zero after each burn, then starts filling again.
